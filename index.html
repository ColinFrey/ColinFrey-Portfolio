About

“Hi! I’m an artist based in Baton Rouge, specializing in traditional methods such as acrylic painting and canvas work. While my foundation is rooted in these classic techniques, I love experimenting with a variety of mediums and approaches to develop my style and push my work in new directions. I am currently pursuing a degree in Art & Design at Louisiana State University, where I look forward to growing as an artist and building a lasting career in the arts.”
